Mental Toughness Assessment

This assessment is designed by Building Mind Psychology to help understand how well an individual manages stress, pressure, and emotional challenges. Mental toughness plays an important role in emotional stability, decision-making, and resilience during difficult situations.

Please read each statement carefully and select the option that best describes how you usually feel or behave. There are no right or wrong answers.

Assessment Scoring Guide

  • Total Score Range: 5 – 25
  • Interpretation:
    1. 5 – 10 : Low Mental Toughness – May require support in managing stress.
    2. 11 – 17 : Moderate Mental Toughness – Coping ability is present but inconsistent.
    3. 18 – 25 : High Mental Toughness – Strong emotional resilience and confidence.

Note: This assessment is for self-awareness purposes and does not replace professional diagnosis.
